About EcoEats

EcoEats is an innovative project that aims to reduce food waste by providing users with recipes based on the ingredients they have on hand. The app allows users to take a photo of their fridge contents, extract the items from the image, and generate recipes accordingly. It is designed to help users make the most out of what they have, thus contributing to a more sustainable and eco-friendly lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Photo-based ingredient extraction
  • Recipe generation based on available ingredients
  • Sustainable living by reducing food waste
  • Modern and intuitive user interface
